About Me

I am currently a research scientist at Facebook. I obtained my Ph.D. from the University of Michigan - Ann Arbor, working with Prof. Scott Mahlke in the Computer Engineering Laboratory (CELAB). Before joining UMich in August 2014, I received my B.Eng. degree of Electrical Engineering in Zhejiang University, China.

If you need more information about my current work with Deep Neural Networks (DNNs), please Contact Me.
